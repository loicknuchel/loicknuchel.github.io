Here are a few thoughts about all of my experiences:
- Networking is really important, but you need a pretext for doing it. For that, freelancing and launching a startup are awesome. I saw myself quite lazy about it when I was in a full time contract. I lost a big part of my network when I was very comfortable at Criteo.
- Paradoxically, some of less promising experiences were the most interesting ones and some awesome experiences on paper turned pretty bad. Maybe when the expectations a low you can be happier. Or maybe when the competence gap is big, you can make awesome things.
